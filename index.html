About Me

As an experienced professional transitioning into software engineering, I'm eager to apply my skills and passion to an industry that embraces innovation and creativity. My recent graduation from App Academy equipped me with a strong foundation in web development and the ability to create full-stack applications using widely-adopted technologies such as React, Node.js, Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL and MongoDB.

I'm not afraid to fail and I'm happiest learning new things. Throughout the bootcamp, I was consistently pushed to gain fluency in new languages, technologies and libraries, under deadlines that at first seemed impossible. This experience sharpened my skills in problem-solving, debugging, and project management, but even more importantly, it gave me the confidence to enthusiastically tackle the seemingly impossible!

Looking ahead, I am excited to continue developing new projects that showcase my abilities and address diverse needs. I have a keen interest in building full-stack applications that focus on robust user authentication and encryption for sensitive materials, particularly within the financial sector. By leveraging my past experiences and newfound expertise, I am confident that I can make a meaningful impact in any software engineering role.
